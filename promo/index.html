How to redeem

A customer qualifies for the discount if any of the following are true:

  1. The customer shows their Menuva order summary to staff, or
  2. The customer states that Menuva was used, or
  3. Staff can see or verify that Menuva was used to place the order.
  1. 5.1 Staff may not refuse the discount solely because the customer cannot show their device, provided one of the alternative verification conditions is met.
  2. 5.2 Redemption is subject to operational feasibility. If a venue’s till or POS system cannot apply the discount due to technical constraints, the venue may decline to apply it. Menuva is not responsible for such issues.
